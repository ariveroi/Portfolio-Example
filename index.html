About me

I'm Agustin Rivero a Software Developer

I am a hard worker, lover of new challenges and eager to learn and improve every day. My goal is to learn the latest technologies to improve and facilitate, as much as possible, the business and lives of those who need it.

I have worked in different sectors throughout my professional career. I started as a cybersecurity consultant at Deloitte, where I learned the basic security fundamentals that companies should have.

Later, I had an internship at Amazon, where I was able to enter the world of the "Cloud", using Amazon Web Services. During this internship, I developed a "Full-Stack" application, developing the front-end with Vue.js and all the back-end using AWS services such as Lambda, DynamoDB, API Gateway or SES, integrating the deployment in a single file using the native AWS Cloud Development Kit (CDK).

I have also worked, briefly, at Accenture as a Cloud Architect in the AWS team.

During these years, on my own I have been developing small projects to learn, improve and consolidate the knowledge I have acquired over the years.

Currently working at NTS as a Salesforce Developer.

Eager to keep learning and to give the best of me every day.
